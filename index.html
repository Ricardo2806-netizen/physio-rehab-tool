ⓘ Getting Started

  1. Allow camera access when prompted by your browser
  2. Position yourself 3-5 feet from the camera
  3. Ensure your full body (or upper body for seated exercises) is visible
  4. Choose an exercise that matches your mobility level from the Exercises page
  5. Press the Start button when ready to begin
